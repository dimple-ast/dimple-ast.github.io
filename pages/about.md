My roots trace back to a quaint village in Haryana, where I spent my formative years and completed my early education. I pursued my Bachelor’s and Master’s degrees in Physics at Kurukshetra University. Driven by a deep-seated fascination with the cosmos, I ventured to the serene hills of Nainital, Uttarakhand, to begin my research odyssey in 2018 at the Aryabhatta Research Institute of observational sciencES (ARIES). There, I earned my Ph.D., exploring the enigmatic cosmic events, high-energy transients through my thesis “Multi-wavelength studies of gamma-ray bursts (GRBs) and their associated counterparts,” thereby enriching our comprehension of some of the most intense energetic occurrences in the cosmos. Apart from research, I feel a deep connection with cultural. I love to travel and to explore other cultures and various traditions. I love dancing which is my stress buster.
