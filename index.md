### Hi, I'm [Dimple](https://github.com/dimple-ast) 👋
I am a Post Doctoral Fellow at the Chennai Mathematical Institute (CMI), India and soon I will join University of Birmingham, UK. I'm working in high energy transients, particularly Gamma-Ray Bursts (GRBs), gravitational waves, and supernovae. GRBs are the intense flashes of gamma-rays that can last from milliseconds to thousands of seconds. These are the brightest objects that can outshine the whole universe for a few seconds. GRBs have uniform distribution over the sky and have Redshift distribution from 0.07 to 9.4. Studying these events at high redshifts can probe the star formation history of the universe, which motivates me to explore them.
